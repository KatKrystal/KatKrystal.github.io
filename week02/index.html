"As We May Think" Response

         When I read "As We May Think" by Vannecar Bush, I was under the impression that this was written recently. For most of the essay, I was like "What is this dude talking about, this stuff already exists". I got to the memex part and was confused as to why he was describing a computer, so I checked the date it was written and my mind was blown. This guy managed to literally predict the future, so much so that I didn't even realize he was from the past. His ideas to use science to better the human and accumulate knowledge were spot on in what we needed to progress ourselves. I am mostly just impressed with this guy for being so spot on.
